Below are some amazing products and ideas focused on tackling food waste issues.

Love Food Hate Waste

Visit Vancouver's 'Zero Food Waste' educational site and learn more
about the effects of food waste and how you can help.

Overcooked

An app for businesses, farmers, grocery stores - or anyone for that matter - to post their surplus foods and help connect charities with hungry mouths.

Plenty of Thyme

Grocery manager to help users save food and money and to become more aware of how much waster they produce

Green Deals

Green Deals is a web-based platform which allows grocery stores to advertise and sell food items before they expire.

Use It Up

An informational app that shows recipes for foods at different stages of ripeness, as well as storage and food revival tips.
